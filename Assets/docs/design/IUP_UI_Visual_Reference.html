Section 6.1 — Staff briefing screen (pre-session)
GRIDHUB SECTOR MANAGEMENT — STAFF INDUCTION PORTAL  |  INTERNAL USE ONLY
OPERATOR BRIEFING — SECTOR CONTROL DIVISION
You have been assigned as Duty Operator for Sector Management. Your responsibility is to monitor system health and respond to operational incidents as they arise. You will be supported by ARIA, an AI advisory system. ARIA's recommendations are non-binding.
SYSTEM HEALTH INDICATORS
Your panel displays four indicators: Stability (operational integrity of sector infrastructure), Resources (available staffing, budget and reserve capacity), Workload (current alert burden on the operational team), and Public Confidence (external stakeholder and public sentiment).
ARIA ADVISORY SYSTEM
ARIA provides recommendations based on available sensor data. ARIA's analysis may be incomplete or delayed. You are not required to follow ARIA's recommendations.
OPERATIONAL NOTES
Each incident will present response options. Select the response you judge most appropriate. Session data will be logged for internal review.
This briefing does not constitute a full operational manual. Refer to GRIDHUB Technical Documentation Series 4 for complete system specifications. [Document unavailable — access restricted]
Fig 3 — Staff briefing screen. Styled as internal onboarding memo. Note the intentional gap: technical documentation is referenced but "access restricted" — this is productive uncertainty, not missing content.
